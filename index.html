    About Us

    Kalam IAS Academy was founded by a team of committed individuals who believe that the traditional way of UPSC preparation needs to be changed.

    UPSC civil services exam is not merely a game of content rather It’s a test of your holistic persona. So, we not only focus on what but also on how. We do not merely feed student of relevant content, rather we aim to inculcate the right strategy to approach the exam. We understand that it’s a long journey where one needs to be skillful as well as patient. Thus, it’s very important that personal attention is given to strengthen the strengths and weaken the weaknesses of a student.

    We firmly believe that everyone should be empowered to give one’s best on the day of the exam. At Kalam IAS, we promise to be with you always to get the best out of you.

    Peeyush Kumar

    (CEO, Kalam IAS Academy)

    Mr Peeyush Kumar is an alumnus of IIT Kharagpur. He has founded KALAM IAS Academy with a vision to provide customized solution to every aspirant. He often says, " Every aspirant is unique and so should be the mentorship, tailored to her specific needs".

    He has years of teaching experience in the field of Civil Services. He is well sought after by the top rankers to improve their ranks in the Civil Services Exam. His innovative pedagogy of "Feedback based Stimulated Learning" and the unique "Face to Face Evaluation" have helped many aspirants realize their dreams.

    M.S. Sharma

    (Managing Director, Kalam IAS Academy)

    Mr M.S. Sharma has completed his B.Tech from IIT Kharagpur. His extensive experience as a mentor and former Government of India Officer motivated him to cofound the Kalam IAS Academy. He aims to redefine the Civil Services Preparation with his “individual centric empowerment “approach.

    Mr M.S. Sharma firmly believes in the philosophy of teaching a person how to fish rather than doling it out. He often says, "One can never know all the answers but still with the right approach, one can give his/her best to maximize one’s potential. Inculcation of such an approach requires curated approach". The team at Kalam IAS works with the same spirit under his guidance.
